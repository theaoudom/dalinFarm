Product Description

Our organic bananas are carefully cultivated in our sustainable farm using traditional farming methods combined with modern organic practices. Each bunch is hand-picked at the optimal ripeness to ensure the best flavor and nutritional value.

Growing Process

We use natural fertilizers and pest control methods to maintain the health of our banana plants. Our irrigation system is designed to conserve water while providing optimal growing conditions. No artificial ripening agents or chemicals are used in any stage of the growing process.

Storage Tips

  • Store at room temperature until ripe
  • Once ripe, can be refrigerated to slow ripening
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Store away from other fruits to prevent accelerated ripening
